# OctoAcme Project Management Docs

## Overview

OctoAcme uses a structured, iterative, and evidence-driven approach to project management. Our process emphasizes customer-first value, clear ownership, measurable outcomes, risk management, and fast feedback. We support projects from idea to release and improvement through roles, artifacts, and simple rituals.

## Project Management Philosophy

OctoAcme follows a lifecycle-based approach spanning five core phases: **Initiation**, **Planning**, **Execution**, **Release**, and **Close & Retrospective**. During initiation, teams validate business needs and align stakeholders around a lightweight Project One-pager that defines the problem, goals, success metrics, and resource needs. Once approved, the planning phase breaks work into shippable increments with prioritized backlogs, estimated scope, defined acceptance criteria, and documented dependencies.

Execution and delivery are coordinated through a consistent rhythm of synchronization and communication. Teams conduct daily standups, weekly delivery syncs, and regular demos at sprint or milestone boundaries. Work flows through a project board with standard columns, and pull requests are kept small with clear acceptance criteria. Quality is embedded throughout via unit tests, integration tests, end-to-end smoke tests, security scanning, and manual QA when needed.

Governance and risk management are central to OctoAcme's operations. Clear roles define accountability—**Project Managers** coordinate schedules and communications, **Product Managers** prioritize and measure outcomes, **Developers** implement features and collaborate on design, and **QA/Testing** validates quality. Risks are monitored continuously through a risk register, with a three-level escalation path ensuring blockers are resolved appropriately.

Finally, OctoAcme institutionalizes continuous improvement through structured retrospectives held after each sprint, release, or milestone. Action items flow into the backlog with named owners and due dates. This cycle of learning and incremental refinement, combined with data-informed decision-making and psychological safety, enables OctoAcme teams to deliver reliably while building institutional knowledge.
